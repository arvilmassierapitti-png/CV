A public programme combining scientific excellence, hands-on field practice and close ties with industry (Naval Group, Thales...).

The PEI-A Pathway

I am currently in the integrated preparatory cycle (PEI-A). This is a fundamental and multidisciplinary 2-year scientific programme, complemented by courses oriented towards the engineering profession.

Geipi Polytech Entrance Exam

Admission is through the national Geipi Polytech entrance exam after a General Baccalaureate (registration via Parcoursup). Once the 2 preparatory years are validated, students move directly into the 3-year engineering cycle.
